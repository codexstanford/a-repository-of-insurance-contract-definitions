These are contents that are individually worth more than £2,000 that you have named on your policy. Furniture which isn’t antique and home appliances don’t need to be named, even if they cost more than £2,000. By home appliance we mean an item used for household tasks, like a washing machine or dishwasher.

Specified items are items that have been individually identified to us and are shown in your schedule.
