The National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 and any amendments to it.
---
Means an Act of Parliament and any substitution of, amendment to, or replacement of that Act and any statutory regulation made under that Act.
---
means any Act of the New Zealand Parliament in force at the commencement of the period of insurance, or which comes into force during the period of insurance, and any substitution of, amendment to, replacement of, or any statutory regulation made under such Act.
