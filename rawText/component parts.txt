By way of example, for a roof, the individual component parts or items of the roof would include, as applicable: a truss, a rafter, a section of decking, underlayment, sheathing or drip edge, a shingle, a tile or other outer covering, a pipe jack, a vent or a skylight, and all other individual items or parts. Each of these individual items or parts would also be component parts of the dwelling.
