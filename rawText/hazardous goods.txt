The term Hazardous Goods means those goods covered by the following regulations • The Carriage of Dangerous Goods and Use of Transportable Pressure Equipment Regulations 2009 • The Carriage of Dangerous Goods and Use of Transportable Pressure Equipment Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2010 • The ‘Approved List of Dangerous Substances’ as published by the Health and Safety Executive or any re-enactment or replacement of such regulations and any other legislation of similar intent (including subsequent legislation) if applicable.
---
Explosive substances, gases, flammable liquids or solids, toxic substances, infectious substances and radioactive material.
