means an amount ordered by a New Zealand court under section 32 of the Sentencing Act 2002 to be paid to the victim of an offence. Reparation does not include: a. reparation arising from prosecution of an offence under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015; or b. damages, court costs, fines, penalties, any other form of criminal sanction, nonpecuniary relief, taxes, any payment deemed to be unlawful to insure against; or c. your legal defence costs or expenses in relation to an offence.
