Domiciliary hospitalization means medical treatment for an illness/disease/injury which in the normal course would require care and treatment at a hospital but is actually taken while confined at home under any of the following circumstances: - the condition o f the patient is such that he/she is not in a condition to be removed to a hospital, or - the patient takes treatment at home on account o f non availability o f room in a hospital.
