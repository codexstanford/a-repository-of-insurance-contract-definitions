(i) For stays of at least 24 hours, Formal Hospital Admission is the formal acceptance by a hospital or other inpatient health care facility of a patient who is to be provided with a room, board as well as continuous nursing service in the hospital in which the patient resides at least overnight. (ii) For stays of less than 24 hours in case of Surgical Procedures, Formal Hospital Admission is the formal document indicating that the patient is provided with nursing services and a bed, despite the fact that s/he does not stay overnight. (iii) For stays of less than 24 hours in case of nonSurgical Procedures, Formal Hospital Admission is the formal document indicating that the patient has entered the hospital for less than 24 hours for chemotherapy, radiotherapy or dialyses treatment for less than 24 hours. The patient enters for Treatment and leaves after treatment.
