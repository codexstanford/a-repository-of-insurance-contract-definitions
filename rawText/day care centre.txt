A day care centre means any institution established for day care treatment of illness and/or injuries or a medical setup with a hospital and which has been registered with the local authorities, wherever applicable, and is under supervision of a registered and qualified medical practitioner AND must comply with all minimum criterion as under – i) has qualified nursing staff under its employment; ii) has qualified medical practitioner/s in charge; iii) has fully equipped operation theatre of its own where surgical procedures are carried out; iv) maintains daily records of patients and will make these accessible to the insurance company's authorized personnel.
