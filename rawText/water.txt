means water (H2O) alone, whether in liquid, frozen, or vapor form, or any liquid, sewage or sludge which contains water, whether or not contaminated or combined with or containing other chemicals, particulates, microorganisms or impurities. Water includes but is not limited to rain, snow, sleet, slush, ice, dampness, vapor, condensation, moisture, steam and humidity.
