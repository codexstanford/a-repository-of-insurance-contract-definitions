means that part of the land on which the dwelling which constitutes your home is situated, which is used by you, by members of your family, or by your tenant, for predominantly residential purposes. It does not include: • any part of the land which is used for commercial or farming purposes; or • any part of the land that is more than 150 metres away from a dwelling or garage used for residential purposes, where the home is situated on a property greater than 10,000 square metres in size, and is not serviced by a dedicated town mains water supply.
