The reasonable cost to repair your house to a standard that is reasonably equivalent to its appearance, size, functionality and relative quality when new, but without necessarily reproducing it exactly.
---
The cost of repairing partially damaged property, or, if property is totally Lost or destroyed or uneconomical to repair, the cost of replacing property as new less a deduction for wear, tear or depreciation. (Note: We will pay a reasonable proportion of the total value of a set or pair to repair or replace an item that is part of a set or pair).
