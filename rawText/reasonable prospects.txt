(a) For civil cases, the prospects that you will recover losses or damages (or obtain any other legal remedy which we have agreed to, including an enforcement of judgment), or make a successful defence, must be at least 51%. We, or a preferred law firm on our behalf, will assess whether there are reasonable prospects. (b) For criminal cases there is no requirement for there to be prospects of a successful outcome. (c) For all civil and criminal appeals the prospect of a successful outcome must be at least 51%.
